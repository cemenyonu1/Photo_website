We are Cory and Kayla, brother and sister and the creative duo behind Alexander-Marie Photography. To us, photography is more than just capturing an image, it’s about preserving the feeling of a moment. Whether we are documenting the quiet anticipation of a growing family, the high energy of a wedding day, or the unique character of a beautiful home, our goal remains the same: to tell a story with clarity and heart. We bring a shared intuition and a double perspective to every shoot, ensuring that no detail—big or small—is ever overlooked. From the front porch of your new listing to the first dance of your forever, we’re here to make sure the memory lasts.
